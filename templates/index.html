How Can We Help You?

Our AI-powered chatbot assists with various customer service needs listed below. It’s available 24/7 to help you explore how intent-based chatbots work.

Supported Query Intents:

• cancel_order • change_order • change_shipping_address • check_cancellation_fee • check_invoice • check_payment_methods • check_refund_policy • complaint • contact_customer_service • contact_human_agent • create_account • delete_account • delivery_options • delivery_period • edit_account • get_invoice • get_refund • newsletter_subscription • payment_issue • place_order • recover_password • registration_problems • review • set_up_shipping_address • switch_account • track_order • track_refund

Important Note:

This chatbot operates using a static intent model. It utilizes intent_model.pkl to identify your query type and fetches a predefined response from intents_response.json.

As this is a prototype, The chatbot give accurate first predefined response only for the supported intents above, other replies are not defined for that particular intent. Questions outside these topics may result in incorrect or irrelevant replies.
